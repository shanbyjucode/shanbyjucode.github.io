Dumbbell Hammer Curl


Setup

  • Stand tall with a dumbbell in each hand, arms fully extended by your sides.
  • Keep your palms facing each other (neutral grip) throughout the exercise.
  • Keep your elbows close to your torso and feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Engage your core and keep your spine neutral.

Execution

  • Curl Up: Exhale and curl both dumbbells upward simultaneously while keeping your palms facing each other.
  • Squeeze: Contract your biceps at the top of the movement for maximum effect.
  • Lower with Control: Inhale and slowly return the dumbbells to the starting position.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Keep Wrists Neutral: Avoid rotating your wrists during the lift.
  • No Swinging: Perform the movement slowly and with control.
  • Stable Core: Brace your abs to maintain a strong and upright posture.
  • Full Range of Motion: Lower the dumbbells completely before the next rep.

Benefits of Hammer Curl

  • Targets Biceps and Brachialis: Helps build thicker arms by engaging deeper arm muscles.
  • Improves Grip Strength: Neutral grip engages the forearms more.
  • Versatile Exercise: Can be performed seated, standing, or with alternating arms.
  • Low Risk of Injury: Natural wrist position reduces strain on joints.
