Cable Fly


Setup

  • Attach single-grip handles to the high pulleys of a cable machine.
  • Stand in the center of the machine with feet staggered for stability.
  • Grab the handles and step slightly forward with arms outstretched but slightly bent.
  • Keep your chest lifted and shoulders down, with a slight lean forward.

Execution

  • Fly Movement: Bring the handles together in front of your chest in a wide arc.
  • Peak Contraction: Squeeze your chest at the center for a second.
  • Return: Slowly open your arms back to the starting position while controlling the motion.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Elbow Slightly Bent: Keep a gentle bend to reduce joint strain.
  • Don't Swing: Use your chest, not momentum, to move the handles.
  • Full Range: Go for a deep stretch at the top for maximum engagement.

Benefits of Chest Cable Fly

  • Isolated Chest Activation: Minimizes shoulder/tricep involvement to better isolate the pecs.
  • Constant Tension: Cables provide consistent resistance throughout the range of motion.
  • Better Stretch & Contraction: Excellent for hypertrophy and shaping the inner chest.
