Leg Curl


Setup

  • Adjust the leg curl machine so that the roller pad rests just above your heels, on the back of your lower legs (calves).
  • Lie face down on the bench with your knees just past the edge of the pad.
  • Grip the handles or sides of the bench to stabilize your upper body.
  • Keep your hips pressed down and legs fully extended before starting.

Execution

  • Curl: Exhale and curl your legs up toward your glutes by flexing your knees.
  • Squeeze: At the top of the movement, pause briefly and squeeze your hamstrings.
  • Lower: Inhale as you slowly return the weight to the starting position in a controlled manner.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Full Range of Motion: Make sure to fully extend and fully contract your legs on each rep.
  • Slow Eccentric: Lower the weight slowly to maximize hamstring activation.
  • Stable Core: Keep your torso and hips pressed into the bench to avoid momentum.
  • Focus: Visualize your hamstrings doing the work to enhance mind-muscle connection.

Benefits of Leg Curl

  • Hamstring Strength: Isolates and strengthens the hamstring muscles effectively.
  • Knee Joint Support: Improves stability and function of the knee joint.
  • Injury Prevention: Balanced leg development reduces injury risks during athletic activity.
  • Leg Aesthetics: Enhances the shape and tone of your lower body.
