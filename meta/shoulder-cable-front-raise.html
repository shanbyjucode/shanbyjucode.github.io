Cable Front Raise


Setup

  • Attach a single handle to the lowest setting of a cable machine.
  • Stand facing away from the machine, with the cable between your legs.
  • Grab the handle with an overhand grip using one hand.
  • Stand tall with a slight bend in your knees and your core engaged.
  • Position your working arm in front of your thigh, elbow slightly bent.

Execution

  • Raise: Exhale and lift the handle in front of you to shoulder height, keeping your arm straight.
  • Pause: Briefly hold the top position and squeeze the front deltoid.
  • Lower: Inhale as you slowly lower the handle back to the starting position with control.
  • Repeat: Perform the desired number of reps before switching sides.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Controlled Movement: Avoid using momentum or swinging the weight.
  • Neutral Wrist: Keep your wrist straight to reduce strain.
  • Shoulder Isolation: Focus on lifting with the deltoid, not your traps or arms.
  • Core Tight: Engage your abs to prevent lower back strain.

Benefits of Cable Front Raise

  • Front Deltoid Activation: Specifically targets the anterior delts for shoulder development.
  • Constant Tension: Cable provides consistent resistance throughout the movement.
  • Improved Aesthetics: Enhances shoulder roundness and upper body definition.
  • Better Control: Smooth cable resistance helps with controlled, safe reps.
