Seated Cable Fly


Setup

  • Sit down on a cable machine bench with your back firmly supported.
  • Adjust the pulleys to shoulder height and grab the handles with a neutral grip.
  • Keep your feet flat on the ground and slightly arch your back.
  • Extend your arms outward with a slight bend in the elbows.

Execution

  • Fly Inward: Bring the handles together in a wide arc motion until they meet in front of your chest.
  • Peak Contraction: Squeeze your chest muscles at the peak of the movement.
  • Return: Slowly return to the starting position, keeping the motion controlled.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Elbow Bend: Maintain a slight bend in your elbows throughout to protect joints.
  • Controlled Motion: Avoid letting the weights pull your arms back too quickly.
  • Posture: Keep your back flat and core engaged at all times.

Benefits of Seated Cable Fly

  • Chest Isolation: Great for isolating and activating the pectoral muscles.
  • Constant Tension: Cables maintain resistance through the entire range of motion.
  • Improved Symmetry: Helps develop balanced chest muscles.
