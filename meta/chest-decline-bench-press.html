Decline Bench Press


Setup

  • Position yourself on a decline bench with your feet secured under the padded supports.
  • Grip the barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width using an overhand grip.
  • Unrack the bar and hold it directly above your lower chest with arms fully extended.

Execution

  • Lower: Slowly lower the bar in a controlled motion to your lower chest, keeping your elbows at about a 45-degree angle.
  • Press: Push the bar back up in a straight line until your arms are fully extended again.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Benefits of Decline Bench Press

  • Lower Chest Emphasis: Specifically targets the lower portion of the pectoral muscles.
  • Improves Overall Chest Development: Balances your upper and lower chest aesthetics and strength.
  • Increased Lifting Power: Allows heavier loads due to better leverage compared to flat or incline presses.
