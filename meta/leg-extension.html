Leg Extension


Setup

  • Sit on the leg extension machine with your back firmly against the backrest.
  • Position your feet under the padded lever, ensuring the pad rests just above your ankles.
  • Adjust the machine settings so your knees align with the pivot point of the lever.
  • Grip the handles for support and keep your back straight.

Execution

  • Extend: Slowly extend your legs until they are almost straight but avoid locking your knees.
  • Pause: Hold the position briefly to maximize quadriceps contraction.
  • Lower: Slowly lower the weight back to the starting position with control.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Controlled Movement: Avoid using momentum; perform slow and steady reps.

  • Adjust Weight: Choose a resistance level that challenges you but allows proper form.
  • Full Range of Motion: Fully extend and lower to engage the quads effectively.

Benefits of Leg Extension

  • Quad Strength: Isolates and strengthens the quadriceps muscles.
  • Knee Stability: Supports the knee joint and can aid injury prevention.
  • Muscle Definition: Helps improve the tone and appearance of the front thighs.
  • Rehabilitation: Useful for controlled strengthening post knee injuries.
