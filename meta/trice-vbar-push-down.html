V Bar Push Down


Setup

  • Attach a V-bar to the high pulley on a cable machine.
  • Stand facing the machine with feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Grasp the V-bar with a neutral grip (palms facing each other), elbows tucked close to your sides.
  • Lean forward slightly from the hips and brace your core.

Execution

  • Push the V-bar down by extending your elbows until your arms are fully straightened.
  • Squeeze your triceps at the bottom of the movement.
  • Return to the starting position slowly, keeping tension on the triceps.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps with controlled movement.

Tips

  • Keep your elbows fixed in place throughout the movementâ€”do not let them flare out.
  • Do not use momentum or lean excessively to push the weight down.
  • Control the weight on the way up to maintain muscle tension.

Benefits

  • Effectively isolates the triceps muscles.
  • Helps build arm size and definition.
  • Improves strength for pressing movements like bench press or overhead press.

Muscles Worked

  • Primary: Triceps Brachii
  • Secondary: Anconeus, Forearms (stabilizers)
