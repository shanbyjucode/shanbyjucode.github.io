Double Biceps Curl


Setup

  • Stand upright with a dumbbell in each hand and arms fully extended at your sides.
  • Use a supinated grip (palms facing forward).
  • Keep your feet shoulder-width apart and your core engaged.
  • Maintain a straight back and relaxed shoulders.

Execution

  • Curl: Bend your elbows and lift both dumbbells simultaneously toward your shoulders.
  • Contract: Squeeze your biceps at the top of the movement without letting your elbows drift forward.
  • Lower: Slowly lower the weights back to the starting position in a controlled manner.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Avoid Swinging: Keep your torso still and avoid using momentum.
  • Full Range: Extend your arms fully at the bottom for maximum stretch.
  • Keep Elbows Tucked: Avoid flaring elbows out or moving them forward.

Benefits of Double Biceps Curl

  • Builds Mass: Simultaneous curls help target both arms equally.
  • Symmetry: Promotes balanced development between both biceps.
  • Simple & Effective: Easy to perform and very efficient for hypertrophy.
