Cable Row


Setup

  • Attach a close-grip or wide-grip handle to a seated cable row machine.
  • Sit down with your feet braced against the platform and knees slightly bent.
  • Grab the handle with both hands and sit upright with your back straight.
  • Pull the handle toward your torso to take the slack out of the cable before beginning.

Execution

  • Pull: Drive your elbows back and pull the handle towards your abdomen.
  • Squeeze: Pause and squeeze your shoulder blades together at the end of the movement.
  • Return: Slowly extend your arms forward under control, maintaining good posture.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Neutral Spine: Keep your back straight and avoid rounding your shoulders.
  • Full Range: Let your arms extend fully in front to stretch the lats before pulling.

Benefits of Cable Row

  • Thicker Back: Targets the middle back and rear delts for width and density.
  • Improves Posture: Strengthens the upper back muscles that support good posture.
  • Great for All Levels: Smooth cable resistance is beginner-friendly yet challenging.
