Pec Fly


Setup

  • Adjust the seat of the pec fly machine so that the handles are at chest level.
  • Sit down with your back firmly against the pad and feet flat on the ground.
  • Grasp the handles with your palms facing forward and arms slightly bent at the elbows.
  • Keep your chest up, core engaged, and maintain a neutral spine.

Execution

  • Contract: Bring the handles together in front of your chest by squeezing your pecs.
  • Hold: Briefly pause at the peak contraction point with your hands close together.
  • Return: Slowly return the handles back to the starting position in a controlled motion.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Controlled Movement: Avoid using momentum; keep the motion smooth and controlled.
  • Maintain Tension: Don’t let the weights rest between reps—keep constant tension on the chest.
  • Breath Control: Exhale as you bring the handles together and inhale as you return to start.

Benefits of Pec Fly

  • Chest Isolation: Specifically targets the pectoral muscles for better definition and growth.
  • Joint-Friendly: Machine provides support and stability, reducing risk of injury.
  • Mind-Muscle Connection: Enhances focus on chest contraction for improved effectiveness.
