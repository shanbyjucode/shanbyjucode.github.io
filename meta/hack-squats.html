Hack Squat


Setup

  • Place a barbell on your upper traps (high-bar position) and stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Brace your core and keep your chest lifted.
  • Position your hands evenly on the bar, gripping it firmly.
  • Unrack the barbell and take a step back into your squat stance.

Execution

  • Lower: Begin the squat by pushing your hips back and bending your knees.
  • Depth: Lower down until your thighs are parallel to the floor or slightly below, keeping knees in line with toes.
  • Drive Up: Press through your heels to return to the starting position.
  • Repeat: Perform the movement with control for the desired reps.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Controlled Breathing: Inhale while lowering, exhale while driving up.
  • Back Position: Maintain a neutral spine throughout — don’t round your lower back.
  • Foot Placement: Adjust stance slightly wider or narrower based on comfort and mobility.
  • Use a Spotter: For safety, especially when lifting heavy.

Benefits of Hack Squat

  • Max Muscle Engagement: Works quads, hamstrings, glutes, calves, and core.
  • Strength Gains: Great for building overall lower body power.
  • Hormonal Boost: Heavy compound lifts like these trigger testosterone and growth hormone production.
  • Mental Toughness: Demanding sets help build endurance and grit.
