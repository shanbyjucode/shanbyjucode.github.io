Stiff Leg


Setup

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a barbell in front of your thighs with an overhand grip.
  • Keep your knees slightly bent and your back straight.
  • Engage your core and retract your shoulder blades slightly.

Execution

  • Lower the barbell slowly by hinging at the hips, keeping the bar close to your legs.
  • Stop when you feel a strong stretch in your hamstrings or when your back is almost parallel to the floor.
  • Return to the starting position by driving your hips forward and lifting your torso upright.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps while maintaining control.

  • Use lighter weights until you master form to avoid injury.
  • Move slowly and focus on hamstring stretch and contraction.

Benefits

  • Targets the hamstrings and glutes effectively.
  • Improves posterior chain strength.
  • Enhances flexibility and stability in the hips.

Muscles Worked

  • Primary: Hamstrings
  • Secondary: Glutes, Lower Back, Calves
