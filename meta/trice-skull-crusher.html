Skull Crusher


Setup

  • Lie flat on a bench with your feet firmly planted on the ground.
  • Hold an EZ curl bar (or dumbbells) with a narrow grip, arms extended straight up over your chest.
  • Keep your elbows directly above your shoulders and close to your head throughout the movement.
  • Engage your core to maintain stability.

Execution

  • Lower: Slowly bend your elbows to bring the weight down towards your forehead.
  • Control: Keep your upper arms stationary and only move your forearms.
  • Stretch: Lower the weight until your forearms are just past 90 degrees.
  • Push: Extend your arms to raise the weight back to the starting position.

Tips for Effectiveness

  • Elbow Position: Keep elbows locked in place to isolate the triceps effectively.
  • Controlled Movement: Avoid fast or jerky motions to prevent injury.
  • Range of Motion: Don't lower the bar too far beyond your forehead.
  • Breathing: Inhale while lowering, exhale as you push back up.

Benefits of Skull Crushers

  • Triceps Development: Targets all three heads of the triceps.
  • Strength Gains: Builds pushing strength for compound lifts.
  • Arm Toning: Helps define and shape the upper arms.
  • Versatility: Can be done with EZ bars, dumbbells, or cables.
